🔗 How URL Analysis Works

  1. The URL is fetched server-side (no CORS issues)
  2. Visible article text is extracted via HTML parsing
  3. Text is classified with a BERT-based model, then cross-checked against live web evidence
  4. Verdict, confidence, and article title/snippet are returned

⚠️ Known Limitations

  • Paywalled articles will not be accessible
  • JavaScript-rendered (SPA) pages may extract little text
  • Very short articles (< 100 characters) give low confidence
  • The fetch request uses a neutral User-Agent header
