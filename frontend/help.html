Help & Tips

Use these notes to get the cleanest predictions and to understand how the interface is intended to be read.

Getting started

  1. Register and login
  2. Open Predict and submit text or a URL
  3. Review results, explanations, and history

Understanding results

Labels are Real or Fake with a confidence score. Low confidence means uncertainty, so verify manually when the score sits near the boundary.

Improving quality

  • Use clear, self-contained claims
  • Use full article text instead of headlines where possible

What makes the project stronger

The combined history, explanation, and evidence panels make the application easier to inspect and present in demos or reports.
